# Application Strategy Guide

## Priority Tier System

### Tier 1 — Dream (apply with max effort)
Companies: Sarvam AI, Krutrim AI, Redsage Technologies, Ola AI, Agentive AI startups
Action: Referral-first, customised recruiter message, tailored resume, same-day follow-up

### Tier 2 — Strong Fit (standard apply)
Companies: Well-funded Series A/B Indian AI startups, international Gen AI companies with India offices
Action: Apply via portal, send recruiter note on LinkedIn, prep standard questions

### Tier 3 — Safety Net (volume apply)
Companies: Larger tech companies with ML divisions
Action: Portal apply, ATS-optimised resume, minimal personalisation

## Recruiter Message Template Structure
1. Opening: Name-drop their product/recent launch (shows research)
2. One-line on yourself: IIT Guwahati + strongest project
3. Specific fit: 1 sentence connecting your work to their stack
4. Ask: Short, non-committal CTA ("happy to share more if relevant")

## Interview Pipeline Mental Model
- Round 1 (HR/Screening): 60-second intro, why this company, basics
- Round 2 (Technical): ML fundamentals, coding, system design for AI
- Round 3 (Project Deep-Dive): Walk through HumDial or waste detection end-to-end
- Round 4 (Culture/Founder): Vision alignment, how you work

## Follow-Up Protocol
- Day 0: Apply + send recruiter LinkedIn note
- Day 5: One follow-up email if no response ("Following up on my application")
- Day 10: Final follow-up, then move on
- After interview: Thank-you email within 2 hours, reference one specific thing discussed
